Mixed Berry Smoothie

A refreshing, vibrant, and nutrient-packed smoothie that takes just minutes to prepare. Great for breakfast or a post-workout snack.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Frozen Mixed Berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1 Ripe Banana
  • 1 cup Almond Milk (or any milk of choice)
  • 1/2 cup Greek Yogurt (optional, for extra creaminess)
  • 1 tbsp Honey or Maple Syrup (optional)

Instructions

  1. Add the frozen berries and banana to a blender.
  2. Pour in the milk and add the yogurt and sweetener if using.
  3. Blend on high speed until completely smooth and creamy.
  4. If the smoothie is too thick, add a little more milk and blend again.
  5. Pour into a tall glass and serve immediately.
