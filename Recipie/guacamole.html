Fresh Guacamole

The ultimate creamy avocado dip. This simple recipe focuses on fresh ingredients for the best flavor.

Ingredients

  • 3 Ripe Avocados
  • 1 Lime, juiced
  • 1/2 Red Onion, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup Fresh Cilantro, chopped
  • 1 Roma Tomato, seeded and diced (optional)
  • 1/2 Jalapeño, seeded and minced (optional)
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cut avocados in half, remove pits, and scoop the flesh into a medium bowl.
  2. Use a fork to gently mash the avocado to your desired consistency (chunky or smooth).
  3. Stir in the lime juice immediately to prevent browning.
  4. Add the diced onion, cilantro, tomato, and jalapeño.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Stir gently to combine.
  6. Taste and adjust seasoning or lime juice if needed.
  7. Serve immediately with tortilla chips.
