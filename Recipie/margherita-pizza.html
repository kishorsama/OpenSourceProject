Margherita Pizza

Italian • 45m • Serves 2-3

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450 g) pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup canned crushed tomatoes or pizza sauce
  • 200 g fresh mozzarella, torn into pieces
  • Fresh basil leaves (a handful)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • Optional: a pinch of dried oregano

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 250°C (480°F) with a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  2. On a lightly floured surface, stretch or roll the dough into a 12-inch circle.
  3. Spread the crushed tomatoes evenly over the base, leaving a small border for the crust. Season lightly with salt and pepper (and oregano if using).
  4. Scatter the torn mozzarella pieces across the pizza and drizzle with olive oil.
  5. Bake on the preheated stone or sheet for 8–12 minutes, until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
  6. Remove from the oven, top with fresh basil leaves, slice, and serve immediately.
