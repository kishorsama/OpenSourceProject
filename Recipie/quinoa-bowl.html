Quinoa Power Bowl

A nutrient-dense bowl packed with plant-based protein, healthy fats, and vibrant vegetables. Perfect for a quick lunch or a meal-prep dinner.

Prep 10m
Cook 15m
Serves 2

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Cooked Quinoa
  • 1 ripe Avocado
  • 1/2 cup Cherry Tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • Fresh Lime Juice
  • Salt & Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prepare the base: Place the cooked quinoa in a large serving bowl.
  2. Chop the veggies: Slice the cherry tomatoes in half and pit/slice the avocado.
  3. Assemble: Arrange the tomatoes and avocado over the quinoa.
  4. Dress: Drizzle with olive oil and squeeze fresh lime juice over the top.
  5. Season: Add salt and pepper, toss gently, and serve immediately.
